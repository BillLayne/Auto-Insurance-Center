Frequently Asked Questions

How does an independent agent save me money?

Unlike captive agents who work for one company, we partner with over 10 top-rated insurance carriers. We do the shopping for you, comparing policies and prices to find the perfect fit for your needs and budget, often uncovering savings you wouldn't find on your own.

What do I need to get a quote?

For an auto quote, you'll typically need your driver's license number, vehicle information (VIN), and details about your driving history. For a home quote, we'll need the property address and some basic information about the house. The whole process is quick and we can walk you through it over the phone in about 15 minutes!

Can you help me switch from my current insurer?

Absolutely! We make switching easy. Once we find you a better policy, we'll help you handle all the paperwork to cancel your old policy and ensure there are no gaps in your coverage. Our goal is to make the transition seamless and stress-free.
